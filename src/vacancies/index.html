Vita.io is an innovative IT-startup based in Rotterdam. We believe in ‘people first’. It is our mission to help organisations optimize their human potential and get the right people, in the right place, at the right time. That mentality is also applied to our own organisation (on daily basis)!

As such, we have developed an HR analytics platform. We make data driven SaaS solutions that provides strategic insights based on the knowledge, skills, competencies, sentiment and stress levels of employees.

Vita.io was founded in 2013 and we are on our way to grow massively. That’s why we need you! Are you passionate about technical challenges and smart solutions? Then Vita.io is your place to be!

The people who work here have a wide variety of interests and fascinating life experiences. We are ambitious with an innovative and entrepreneurial mindset.
